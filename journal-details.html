Article Processing Charges (APC)

SSSL International Journals operate under an Open Access model. To provide free, immediate, and permanent access to all articles worldwide, the journal levies an Article Processing Charge (APC) once a manuscript is accepted after peer review. This fee covers typesetting, editing, online hosting, archiving, and indexing administration.

Publication Fee Categories Amount
International Authors (Standard Open Access) $120 USD
Indian Authors (Standard Open Access) ₹3000 INR
Copy / Paper Submission Templates Free of Charge
Payment Instruction Notes
